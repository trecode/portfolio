Bespoke Design

Bespoke design is based on deep learning and understanding a specific user and their needs. It includes reserch, analysis, ideation and prototyping.

During this project, I conducted research using various research methods such as interviews, observation, photographic research, experiment, and questionnaires.

interactive prototype

Low-fi prototype

Creating a low-fi prototype helped me to build up the structure of the story. I was making corrections pretty often but it didn’t take much time thanks to the very basic visual representation. The biggest value that I noticed in making the low-fi prototype is that the mistakes and experiments don’t cost much time.

The interesting finding is that the process of creating even low-fi prototypes causes new ideas and boosts creativity because I was completely immersed in the work, processing the visual data, and as a result getting new ideas.

High-fi prototype

During the work on the high-fi prototype I tried to focus on stimulation and adding aesthetics. It helped me to create more empathy for the main character because now he has a face and expresses emotions.

I also tried to use the animation here and there to make the experience more amusing. I think that the low-fi prototype was a good way to build the structure whereas the high-fi prototype helps to make an engaging experience.
