Product analysis

In this video, I analyze and evaluate popular application software in terms of user experience. Specifically, I'm talking about usability, engagement, and meaning factors that make a product valuable to the user group.

The mobile application that I am going to look at is Runkeeper.

Runkeeper is a fitness-tracking app, that allows users to record their fitness activities and see the corresponding characteristics of this activity like km, duration of the activity, pace, calories burned and so on.
